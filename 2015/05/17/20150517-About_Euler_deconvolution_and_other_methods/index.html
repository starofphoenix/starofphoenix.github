About Euler deconvolution and other methods

Reid, A.B., et al., Magnetic interpretation in three dimensions using Euler deconvolution. Geophysics, 1990. 55(1): p. 80 -91.

整理

werner deconvolution (Hartman et al., 1971; Jain, 1976) fits elementary models to successive segments of a profile and estimates source location, depth, and dip.

Thompson (1982) describes a method which applies Euler’s equation to successive segments of a pole-reduced profile, solves for source position, and obtains an indication of source type.

从欧拉齐次方程的公式出发，这个称为构造指数，也就是可以用来描述场源体形态的参量，确确实实是与场源体所引起场的衰减性有关。

Although the structural-index approach to source description does not include irregular boundaries, each solution employs only data within its window, so that irregular sill-like bodies can be well delineated by the Euler method with an index of 1.0, while irregular contacts are well shown with a zero index.

对于实际数据来说，由于包含了不同结构的场源体，对整个地区采用单一的构造指数肯定是不合适的。

It, therefore, appears that pole reduction prior to Euler deconvolution is unnecessary.
The corollary is that the method cannot and does not yield any dip information
不能得到任何的关于倾向的信息。

Selection criteria and spurious solutions

Where a window does not include any significant gradients or where it includes gradients arising from several sources, the solution has a high uncertainty. Noisy or poorly gridded data sets also affect the solution statistics by degrading the fit. In addition, we observe that the lower the structural index employed, the worse the precision, even when the index used is the appropriate one.
当窗口内没有明显的梯度信息，或者包含了多个场源体的梯度信息时，得到的结果不确定度很很高。

Mushayandebvu, M.F., et al., Magnetic source parameters of two-dimensional structures using extended Euler deconvolution. Geophysics, 2001. 66(3): p. 814.

This allows for the structural index to be correctly chosen on the basis of a priori knowledge about susceptibility and dip.
在存在倾向和磁性信息等先验信息的情况下，可以估算得到正确的构造指数。
先验信息。。。

The estimation of the structural index, which relates to source type (e.g., contact, dike, and point) and controls the fall-off rate of the field with distance from the source, is also addressed by Barbosa et al. (1999). Depth estimates are biased if the wrong index is assumed.
构造指数，与场源体的形态有关，并且其数值本身与场和场源的距离的衰减指数相同。
对于二维数据的应用很有效果啊，感觉。

Hansen, R.O. and L. Suciu, Multiple-source Euler deconvolution. Geophysics, 2002. 67(2): p. 525 -535.

解决多场源体时欧拉反褶积的使用问题。

Phillips, J.D. Two-step processing for 3D magnetic source locations and structural indices using extended Euler or analytic signal methods. in SEG. 2002.

在具有地形起伏的情况下，采用两种方法：欧拉反褶积和解析信号法来来获取场源的位置和构造指数。
在明确知道半空间最顶表面的情况下，观测数据如航磁数据等在表面以上时，得到的场源体深度方向位置可能位于表面以上，这是不符合实际的。因此遇到这种情况时需要进行后续处理，本文中有提到。
最终的结论就是，通过这两种方法的结合，可以对上面说的这种情况进行改正，得到的结果不会出现违反物理规律的情况（场源体处于地形面以上）。并且，通过Hilbert转换得到的梯度数据，与原始数据一起可以增加结果的信息量。

For both methods, the number and reliability of solutions is greatly increased by analyzing generalized Hilbert transform components in addition to the observed magnetic field.

过去的磁场场源定位方法中，构造指数多是作为解释的先验信息来处理的。在欧拉反褶积处理中，构造指数的变化范围可以从0到3，在解析信号振幅方法中，该指数通常选为0或1。当所选择的构造指数太小时，所估计的场源深度相对真实情况会太浅；反之则会太深。

Applied to the Analytic Signal Method

提到了如何利用解析信号法来确定边界位置和场源深度

Salem, A. and D. Ravat, A combined analytic signal and Euler method (AN-EUL) for automatic interpretation of magnetic data. Geophysics, 2003. 68(6): p. 1952 -1961.

Gerovska, D. and M.J. Araúzo-Bravo, Automatic interpretation of magnetic data based on Euler deconvolution with unprescribed structural index. Computers & Geosciences, 2003. 29(8): p. 949-960.

欧拉齐次方程推导出的常用公式：
$$(x-x_0){\frac{\partial F}{\partial x}}+(y-y_0){\frac{\partial F}{\partial y}}+(z-z_0){\frac{\partial F}{\partial z}}=-NF$$
其中，$F$是总场强度，包括正常场$B$和总场异常$A$，即$F=A+B$。$N$就是能够描述场源体形态，代表了场据场源位置距离衰减性的构造指数。

Different authors have used different approximation models of the background in a single window.

  1. 在最早使用欧拉方程确定简单模型的深度是，没有考虑背景场的影响。
    In the first applications of Euler’s equation for determining the depth to simple models from magnetic data (Hood, 1965; Ruddock et al., 1966), the presence of background influence was not considered.
    没有背景场的假设，使得可以使用线性最小二乘方法求解出场源坐标和构造指数。但是这样简单的假设会造成结果很发散，since regional fields or constant offsets due to nearby anomalies are almost always present。
    而为了解决这个问题，需要考虑背景场的影响，Thompson (1982) 在每个窗口内采用场值来代表背景场，也就是背景场的0阶泰勒级数展开。这样就引入了代表背景场的该未知常数和构造指数之间的非线性关系，因此线性最小二乘方法无法直接应用于求解，Thompson (1982) 采用假定构造指数的方式，将该非线性关系剔除掉，解决了2D情况下该问题的求 解。Reid et al., (1990) 将该方法拓展到三维情况下，同样对构造指数采用假定数值，然后求解的模式成为了后面学者普遍采用的方式。
    但是最优的构造指数选择仍然是一个悬而未决的问题。
    In this general case, with the standard method every anomaly has to be isolated from the total grid and the correct index and window size should be chosen, which makes the interpretation too subjective and time consuming.

  2. A widely used acceptance criterion for evaluation of the quality of the solutions is based on the rule that the correct structural index produces the most focused image of the source locations (Thompson, 1982; Reid et al., 1990). Another criterion is based on achieving the lowest correlation between the estimated background for the whole grid and the measured field (Barbosa et al., 1999).
    Barbosa et al. (1999) proved that even when background field is not present, the estimation of the horizontal coordinates of the source are independent of the assumed tentative value of the structural index, but the estimate of the source depth is dependent. The latter conclusion shows that the depth estimates are more sensitive to an imprecisely prescribed structural index.
    即使在不考虑背景场的情况下，场源水平位置的估计是跟构造指数的值没有关系的，但是深度的估计是有关系的，预估的构造指数直接关系到深度估计的准确度。
    This drawback suggests that other methods for linearising Euler’s equation, which do not prescribe the structural index but, conversely, estimate it, should be used.
    因此那些不是给定构造系数，而是将其计算出来的方法，更应该被使用。
    那么如何计算出来呢，前面也提到了，在问题可以被线性求解的时候，可以同时计算得到构造指数。但是由于背景场的存在，导致存在非线性的关系使得不能直接求出SI，因此才有上面的先给定假定SI值，然后求取场源位置坐标。但是也说了，这样的假定值是否准确直接影响到了场源深度值的准确度。因此首先想到的是将该非线性关系去掉，Hsu (2002) 采用观测总场的垂向梯度作为主题进行欧拉方程处理，由于假设背景场场值为常量进行求导后也就不存在了，也就变成线性问题。但是由于需要对场求二阶导数，噪声对结果的影响更大。
    另一个就是本文作者提出的，对背景场采用一阶泰勒级数的模拟：
    $$B(x,y,z) = b_0+b_xx+b_yy+b_zz.$$

Salem, A., et al., Interpretation of magnetic data using an enhanced local wavenumber (ELW) method. Geophysics, 2005. 70(2): p. L7 -L12.

This method uses the traditional and phase-rotated local wavenumbers to produce a linear equation as a function of the model parameters.

Nabighian, M. N., and M. Asten, 2002, Metalliferous mining geophysics: State of the art in the last decade of the 20th century and the beginning of the new millennium: Geophysics, 67, 964–978.

SPI_{TM} method (Thurston and Smith, 1997)

which requires second-order derivatives of the total field and uses a term known as the local wavenumber to provide a rapid estimate of the depth of buried magnetic bodies.
However the method is model dependent. And unfortunately a priori knowledge of the nature of a source is not usually available.
对于局部波数法的改进有(Smith et al., 1998; Thurston et al., 2002)，首先可以使该方法能够对深度和场源的种类都进行估计，而不是首先需要知道场源的形态，再去估算场源的深度。但是改进的方法需要场的三阶导数，这样对于数据质量和滤波处理的要求就非常高。
Salem and Smith (2005) 提出了对利用归一化的局部波数法能对场源深度和其种类进行直接的估计。但是这个方法需要依靠从波数剖面中极大值处获得的场源的水平位置，但是数据中的噪声使得该峰值点的位置较难被确定，并且其不精确值直接影响到所估计的场源深度和场源类型的结果。
然后有了这篇文章：
The ELW method is based on use of the traditional local-wavenumber field and its phase-rotated version, the vertical local-wavenumber field. 而且不需要去计算三阶导数哦。

This combination allows us to calculate the source parameters (for 2D sources) regardless of their model type. Thus, ELW is a 2D modelindependent method and only uses derivatives of the anomalous field up to second order.

经过不太复杂的推导后，可以得到如下的公式：
$$k_xx+k_zz=k_xx_0+k_zz_0.$$
因此，这个公式中不包含构造指数，可以直接求取场源位置$(x_0,z_0)$，在求取之后，在根据其他公式去求取构造指数。
文中给出了一套比较完善的求解流程，可以一定程度上抵消虚假场源结果的出现：

  1. 首先找到局部波数 $k_x$ 的峰值所处位置；
  2. 然后在所有峰值处采用邻近的若干点来计算场源位置；
    所选取点说的多少取决于该峰的宽度、数据质量以及与周围场源体的干扰程度。对于孤立的场源体，可以多选取点数来降低数据的噪声影响；对于多个相邻的场源体，应该选取较少的点数来减少邻近场源的干扰影响。
  3. 紧跟第2步得到的场源位置$(x_0,z_0)$，来计算场源的构造指数。
    对于二维磁异常来说，场源体的构造指数应该介于0~2之间，数据噪声和误差可能影响到计算结果，因此可以设定一个阈值范围&(-0.2~2.2)&，超出范围的构造指数结果则舍弃掉。

Pilkington, M. and P. Keating, The relationship between local wavenumber and analytic signal in magnetic interpretation. Geophysics, 2006. 71(1): p. L1 -L3.

A simple relationship exists between the amplitude of the analytic signal (AS), its spatial derivative, and the local wavenumber (LW). This relationship can be extended to higher-order versions of AS and LW, and for horizontal wavenumber, to three dimensions. It demonstrates the equivalence of AS and LW expressions over simple source geometries that can be used for depth estimation if the source geometry is known. The relationship also illustrates the equivalence, through Hilbert transformation, of recent methods using the AS (Salem et al., 2004; Salem, 2005) and the LW (Thurston et al., 2002). Finally, it shows the equivalence between the iSPI method of Smith et al. (1998) based on the LW and the ANEUL method of Salem and Ravat (2003) based on the AS.
